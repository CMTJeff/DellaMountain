The Idea

Della Mountain is twelve commercial condominiums in Hailey, Idaho, each carrying something rare: a live/work entitlement, pre-approved by the city. The result is a property that adapts to its owner — a workspace, a base, a place for what matters to you.

Three buildings. Twelve units. One uncommon opportunity.

Live / Work

A building that doesn't dictate.

Every unit is a genuine commercial space — a fourteen-foot main level, a grade-level roll-up door, and room for a business, a studio, a workshop, the things you build or keep.

Above it, each unit is pre-approved for an accessory dwelling unit, subject to City of Hailey guidelines — quarters for guests, for staff, for yourself. Finish it now or later; the approval work is already in hand. Use the space as commercial alone, or as both. The choice stays yours.

The Setting

Minutes from everything the Valley offers.

Della Mountain sits on Aviation Drive in Hailey, with easy in-and-out access to the Wood River Valley. Friedman Memorial Airport is close at hand, and Highway 75 connects the length of the Valley north to Ketchum and Sun Valley.

A practical address in a place people work to get to.

  • Friedman Memorial Airport — minutes away
  • Direct Highway 75 access
  • Hailey, in the heart of the Wood River Valley
